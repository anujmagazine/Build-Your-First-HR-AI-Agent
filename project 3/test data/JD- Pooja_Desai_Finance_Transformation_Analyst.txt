9. Finance Transformation Analyst

Location : Bengaluru
Team : Finance Systems

Role Overview
Digitise finance workflows and analytics to accelerate close cycles and improve forecasting accuracy.

Key Responsibilities

Document current state of GL, consolidation and reporting processes.

Build data models and reconciliation scripts in BigQuery.

Develop Power BI dashboards for CFO review.

4 + years in finance or BI roles.

Strong SQL and Excel; exposure to IFRS-17 data helpful.

Analytical mindset and clear communication.

Success Measures

Cut month-end close from T + 7 to T + 3 days.

Improve forecast accuracy by 20 %.
