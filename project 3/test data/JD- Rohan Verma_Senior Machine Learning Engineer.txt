Job Description: Senior Machine Learning Engineer – Sun Life GCC India

Location: Bengaluru, Karnataka, India
Department: Data & Analytics, Global Capability Center
Employment Type: Full-time, permanent

Role Overview
Sun Life’s GCC is scaling advanced analytics that streamline claims, underwriting and customer servicing. The Senior Machine Learning Engineer designs, builds and maintains end-to-end ML solutions that turn enterprise data into measurable business impact.

Key Responsibilities

Collaborate with product owners to frame ML use-cases and success metrics.

Engineer features from structured and unstructured insurance data using Python and SQL.

Develop, train and tune models (NLP, tabular, generative) with TensorFlow or PyTorch.

Deploy models to Google Cloud Vertex AI with robust CI/CD and monitoring.

Integrate LangChain-style agent flows that combine Gemini models with vector stores.

Optimise latency and cost through efficient data pipelines and autoscaling.

Produce clear documentation, notebooks and demo apps for stakeholders.

Mentor junior engineers and champion MLOps best practices across squads.

Required Qualifications

6 + years of hands-on ML engineering experience, including at least two production deployments.

Strong coding skills in Python; solid grasp of software design patterns and Git workflows.

Proven work with GCP services (BigQuery, Cloud Functions, Vertex AI) or similar cloud platforms.

Familiarity with Docker, Kubernetes and Terraform.

Experience applying deep-learning or transformer models to insurance, banking or healthcare data.

Excellent communication skills and a track record of stakeholder engagement.

Preferred

Google Professional ML Engineer certification or equivalent.

Knowledge of actuarial or IFRS-17 data structures.

Prior exposure to data privacy, model governance and regulatory reporting in financial services.

Success Measures (first 12 months)

Launch two ML services that reduce claim processing time by at least 15 percent.

Establish an automated model monitoring dashboard with weekly drift alerts.

Upskill five colleagues through internal workshops and code reviews.
